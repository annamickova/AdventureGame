Press enter to start game
You just came to unknown land.
Your goal is to find 10 liters of fuel for your vehicle.
Almost all animals can take your live or your fuel.
You can pick up all items and use some of them.
On top of your screen you can see current lives
and how much fuel you need to collect.
Game movement is controlled by WASD or arrow keys.
If you press F displays screen with functions,
choose pressing enter or close with F or escape key.
