Welcome to an unknown land!
Your mission is to find 10 liters of milk fuel to power your cat ship.
Beware, almost all animals can take your life or steal your milk.
Explore the land, collect items, and use some of them to assist you.
At the top of the screen, you can keep track of your remaining lives,
the amount of fuel still needed and time.

Control your movement using the WASD keys or arrow keys.
Press 'F' to access the function menu,
where you can choose an option by pressing 'Enter'
or close the menu by pressing 'F' or 'Escape'.
